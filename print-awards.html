The HP Digital Print Excellence Awards
for Asia Pacific & Japan 2017
Unleash your creative credentials across APJ at the HP Digital Print Excellence Awards.
The HP Digital Excellence Awards are one of the industry's most well-respected and comprehensive platforms to show off your innovation and creativity. With the adoption of latex technology, print service providers are now able to work directly with brands to create new and exciting innovations. We are inviting you to submit your most exciting and innovative creations for display, showcasing the limitless possibilities of HP Large Format Sign & Display.

This year, the 8th edition Digital Print Awards for HP Large Format Sign & Display are going digital on HP.com. With a 24/7 online submission system you can submit your entries anytime you need, and your creative and innovative entries will be seen by many industry professionals and market leaders on the site. In due time, the online voting system will be open for your brand owners and friends to vote for your entries. You'll also find the list of winners published on the same site.
